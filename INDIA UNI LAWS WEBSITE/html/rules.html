University Rules & Regulations

Get clear and current information on university policies in India — including attendance requirements, conduct standards, campus rules, and hostel regulations.

VIT Bhopal University

    At VIT Bhopal University, students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be eligible for exams. Attendance is tracked from the start of the semester up to the examination date. Falling below this threshold can result in being debarred from the exam, though approved leaves for medical reasons or official university activities (On Duty) are considered. Regular attendance ensures students stay engaged in classes and meet academic requirements.

IIT Delhi

    At IIT Delhi, students are required to maintain a minimum of 75% attendance for each of their courses to be eligible to appear for examinations. Attendance is tracked throughout the semester, and falling below this threshold may result in being debarred from exams. Approved absences, such as medical leave or participation in official university events, are taken into account, but students are encouraged to attend classes regularly to stay engaged and meet academic requirements.
